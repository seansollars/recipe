Macaroni and cheese

Description

Melted Butter with margarine,with added bread crumbs on hot cooked noodles with parmagain cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 4 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • cup butter, melted
  • 1 sleeve buttery round crackers,crushed

Directions

  1. Pour uncooked noodles in hot water
  2. Add cheese after well cooked
  3. Empty pan and add flour and milk
  4. Mix the butter with the flower
  5. Stir until well stirred
  6. Empty pan into bowl
